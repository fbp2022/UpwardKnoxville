Connect & Community

You do not have to walk alone.

We are building a simple, welcoming community for people across Knoxville and nearby towns who want to keep following Jesus together.

Some folks jump right into conversation. Others prefer to stay quiet and listen for a while. Both are welcome. You will not be pressured to perform or pretend.

How we encourage one another

We share prayer requests, Scripture insights, and practical wisdom from everyday life. We try to be honest, gracious, and grounded in God's Word.
