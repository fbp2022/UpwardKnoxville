About Us

We are neighbors trying to follow Jesus together.

Upward Knoxville is a Christ-centered fellowship for Knoxville and all the surrounding communities. We come from different backgrounds and different seasons of life, but we share one desire: to know Jesus better and follow Him faithfully.

We gather around the Bible, prayer, and honest conversation. We are not trying to build a brand. We are trying to build each other up in Christ.

We believe growth takes time. That is why we study Scripture slowly and carefully, verse by verse, so the meaning stays rooted in God's Word and not in our opinions.

Our heartbeat

We want to encourage one another, pass along biblical wisdom, and offer practical help for everyday life. Some days that means deep study. Some days it means prayer and just being there for each other.

Listeners are welcome

If you are quiet, unsure, or just tired, you are still fully welcome. You can listen, take it in, and go at your own pace. There is no pressure to perform, share, or speak before you are ready.
