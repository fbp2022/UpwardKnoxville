Upward Knoxville is a Christ-centered fellowship initiative for people in Knoxville who want to grow in faith, encourage one another, and pursue life upward toward God.

What this is

A fellowship beginning online and growing with purpose.

Upward Knoxville is not currently a church, chapel, ministry campus, or formal weekly service. Right now, it is a growing fellowship initiative for Christians and those exploring faith in Jesus Christ.

The goal is simple: create a place for biblical conversation, prayer, encouragement, discipleship, and local Christian connection in the Knoxville area.

What we’re building

Faith that moves upward.

Fellowship

A place for believers to encourage one another and build real Christian community.

Scripture

Focused on truth, biblical discussion, and growing in understanding of God’s Word.

Prayer

Creating space to pray for one another and carry burdens together.

Local Connection

Beginning online, with the hope of eventually forming in-person fellowship in Knoxville.

Foundation

Centered on Jesus Christ.

Upward Knoxville exists to point people toward Jesus Christ, encourage obedience to Scripture, and build a community shaped by truth, humility, repentance, prayer, and love for one another.

This fellowship is still in its beginning stage. As it develops, this page will continue to clarify beliefs, structure, and future direction with honesty and care.
