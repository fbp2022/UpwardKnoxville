Greater Knoxville Fellowship

A simple place to open the Bible together and look to Jesus.

We are ordinary believers from Knoxville and nearby towns who want to grow in Christ together. We read Scripture slowly, verse by verse, and we help each other keep walking with the Lord one day at a time.

You do not have to have all the answers. You do not have to be great at speaking. If you want to come listen quietly and know you are not alone, you are welcome with us.

Who this is for

Upward Knoxville is open to men and women of all ages across the greater Knoxville area. Whether you have followed Jesus for years or you are just starting to ask questions, we would be glad to walk with you.

This is not about impressing people. It is about staying faithful to Jesus and His Word together.

What we do together

Our main focus is careful, expository Bible teaching through whole books of Scripture. We slow down, read the passage, look at what it actually says, and let the Word shape us.

Along the way we share wisdom, practical advice, prayers, and encouragement from real life.

What we are not

We are not a church, a sports program, or a formal ministry organization. We are simply people who love Jesus and want to keep growing together in truth, humility, and love.

If you want to come and just listen for a while, that is completely okay. You never have to speak before you are ready.
