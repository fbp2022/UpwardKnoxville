What We Believe

Our beliefs are rooted in Scripture and centered on Jesus.

We do not claim to know everything, but we do want to hold firmly to what God has made clear in His Word.

About God

We believe there is one true God, forever existing as Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. He is Creator over everything, holy in all His ways, and full of mercy and love.

(Genesis 1:1; Deuteronomy 6:4; Matthew 28:19)

About the Bible

We believe the Bible is God's inspired and trustworthy Word. It is our final authority for faith and daily life, and it shows us God's heart, His truth, and His rescue plan in Christ.

(2 Timothy 3:16-17; Psalm 119:105; Hebrews 4:12)

About the Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit is fully God. He convicts, comforts, and empowers believers to live holy lives and serve faithfully. He gives spiritual gifts as He chooses, and His work always points us to Christ-like love and biblical order.

(John 14:16-17; John 16:8; Acts 1:8; 1 Corinthians 12:4-11; Galatians 5:22-23)

About Mankind

We believe every person is made in God's image and carries deep value. We also believe all of us have sinned and cannot fix ourselves apart from God's grace.

(Genesis 1:27; Romans 3:23; Romans 5:12)

About Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by our works. Through His death and resurrection, sinners can be forgiven, made new, and reconciled to God.

(Ephesians 2:8-9; Romans 3:23-26; Romans 5:8-9; John 3:16)

About Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus is fully God and fully man. He was born of a virgin, lived without sin, died for our sins, rose bodily from the grave, and ascended to Heaven. He now intercedes for us and will return in power and righteousness.

(John 1:1-3; Philippians 2:5-11; Luke 1:34-35; Romans 5:8; 1 Corinthians 15:3-4; Revelation 19:11-16)

About the Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ made up of believers everywhere. God calls us to gather, worship, pray, teach the Word, make disciples, and serve one another in love.

(Matthew 16:18; Acts 2:42-47; Ephesians 4:11-13; Hebrews 10:25)

About Human Life

We believe every human life is sacred from conception to natural death because each life is made by God and bears His image. Every person has real dignity and worth.

(Psalm 139:13-16; Jeremiah 1:5; Genesis 9:6)

About Gender

We believe God created people male and female, and that this is part of His good design. Men and women are equal in value before God and both reflect His image.

(Genesis 1:27; Matthew 19:4; Psalm 139:13-16)

About Marriage

We believe marriage is a lifelong covenant between one man and one woman, established by God. It reflects Christ's love and is meant to be marked by faithfulness, love, and honor.

(Genesis 2:24; Matthew 19:4-6; Ephesians 5:31-33)

About Sexuality

We believe God designed sexual intimacy for marriage as Scripture defines it. We also believe the gospel offers mercy, forgiveness, and transformation to anyone who turns to Christ in repentance and faith.

(Romans 1:26-27; 1 Corinthians 6:9-11; Hebrews 13:4; 2 Corinthians 5:17; John 8:11)

About Christ's Return and Eternity

We believe Jesus will return, judge with perfect justice, and reign forever. We believe in the resurrection of all people, eternal life with God for those who are in Christ, and eternal separation for those who reject Him.

(Daniel 12:2; John 5:28-29; Revelation 20-21)
